How do I restore my purchase?

Open the app → Settings → Restore Purchase. Your lifetime unlock is tied to your Apple ID and can be restored on any device signed in to the same account at no extra charge.

How do I back up my data?

Go to Settings → Export Data. This saves a JSON file you can reimport later from Settings → Import Data.

Will my data be lost if I delete the app?

Yes — all data is stored locally on your device. Export a backup before deleting the app.

My streak looks wrong

Streaks are calculated based on your habit's scheduled days. If a habit is set to specific weekdays, off-days don't break your streak. Check the habit's schedule in Edit Habit.
